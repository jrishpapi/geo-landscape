## Frequently Asked Questions

### Q: What is GEO and why do I need a tool for it?

**A**: GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) optimizes your brand's visibility in AI-generated answers. Unlike SEO (which targets search engine rankings), GEO targets citation rates in AI platforms like ChatGPT, Claude, and Perplexity. As zero-click searches become dominant on AI platforms, brands that aren't cited in AI answers effectively don't exist to AI-using customers.

### Q: Can't I just do GEO manually?

**A**: You can, but dedicated GEO platforms provide scale and measurement. Manual GEO involves tracking citation rates across multiple AI engines, benchmarking against competitors, and continuously optimizing content — tasks that are nearly impossible to do manually at scale.

### Q: Is CowTech GEO available now?

**A**: CowTech GEO is currently in development. Visit [cowtech.xyz](https://cowtech.xyz) for the latest status and waitlist information.

### Q: What's the difference between GEO and SEO?

**A**: SEO targets human search engine users through ranking optimization. GEO targets AI engines and LLMs through citation optimization. Key differences:

| Dimension | SEO | GEO |
|-----------|-----|-----|
| Goal | Ranking #1 on Google | Being cited in AI answers |
| Metric | Click-through rate | Citation rate |
| Primary platforms | google.com, bing.com | ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, Perplexity |
| Content preference | Keyword-optimized articles | Evidence-backed structured content |
| Competition model | Zero-sum (only #1 matters) | Collaborative (multiple sources cited) |

### Q: How fast can I see GEO results?

**A**: Typically 4–12 weeks for measurable citation changes, depending on your starting point and competitive landscape. GEO is a medium-term investment, not an overnight fix.
